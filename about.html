About Pulsar Medical

Built by industry insiders. For industry insiders.

Pulsar Medical was founded by filmmakers who recognized the gap between standard medical services and the specific, high-pressure demands of working on set. We brought medical-grade care into the film industry — without disrupting the flow of production.

Our Story

Film-industry veterans who became medical professionals.

Pulsar Medical provides set-savvy medical professionals to film and TV productions all across Ontario. We understand the pace of production because we come from the industry — and we know the difference between medical support that slows a set down and medical support that keeps it moving.

Since 2020, we've been committed to delivering reliable, professional on-site medical care that doesn't just respond to emergencies, but actively helps productions stay compliant with health and safety regulations. No shutdowns. No missed calls. No surprises.

At Pulsar Medical, we bring peace of mind to every shoot — because safety should never be left to chance.
