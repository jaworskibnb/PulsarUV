About Pulsar Medical

Built by industry veterans. Trusted by the industry.

Pulsar Medical was founded to bring the level of medical staffing expected in hospitals to the environments that need it most — film sets, live events, and high-pressure workplaces across Ontario.

Est. 2020
Our Story

Film-industry insiders who became medical professionals

Pulsar Medical was founded during one of the most challenging moments in entertainment history — when productions were scrambling to restart during COVID-19 and the industry urgently needed medical expertise that understood both clinical standards and the pace of a working set.

We came from inside the film industry. We'd worked on sets. We knew the difference between medical support that slows a production down and medical support that keeps it moving. So we built what was missing.

Today, Pulsar Medical provides licensed medical staffing across Ontario — to film and TV productions, live events, corporate functions, and workplace environments. Every deployment operates under physician oversight. Every medic is certified. And our track record speaks for itself: zero production shutdowns across all our engagements.
