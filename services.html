What We Do

Medical expertise built for production and performance.

Our Divisions

Two focused divisions.

Each with deep industry expertise and the same uncompromising standard of care.

Division 01

Film & TV Productions

Pulsar Medical is a trusted provider of experienced set medics for film and television productions. With 18 years of industry film experience, we understand the unique pace and demands of production environments.

Since 2020, we've delivered reliable, professional on-site medical support that ensures immediate care when it's needed most and helps productions remain compliant with health and safety regulations.

  • Licensed physician oversight on every production
  • Set-savvy medics who understand production flow
  • Specialized medical consultants on call
  • Full compliance with health & safety regulations
  • Fully-stocked medical kits (AED, EpiPen, Narcan)
  • 24/7 availability, including last-minute bookings
Division 02

Workplace & Events

Pulsar Medical provides professional on-site medics for concerts, festivals, corporate events, and workplace needs. We ensure the safety and well-being of attendees, performers, and crew in every environment.

Whether it's a music festival, a corporate function, or ongoing workplace medical support, our team brings the same level of preparation and responsiveness that productions rely on.

  • Event & concert medical teams
  • Festival and crowd response specialists
  • Corporate workplace medical support
  • Medical consulting and safety planning
  • Scalable team sizes for any event
  • Full WSIB and insurance coverage
